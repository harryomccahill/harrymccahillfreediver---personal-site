I · The approach

Teach the nervous system,
not just the dive.

As an AIDA Master Instructor, Harry has certified over 100 students — from first breath-holds to competition depth. The method is the same calm that defines his own diving: relaxation first, efficient technique second, depth as the result — never the goal.

  • Relaxation and breath-up — where every dive is actually won
  • Equalisation that scales with depth — Frenzel, then mouthfill
  • Streamlined technique and efficient finning — or none at all
  • Safety, rescue and the mental side of going deep, calmly
II · What you can train

The AIDA ladder —
beginner → instructor.

Full AIDA certification pathway, taught year-round in Dominica. Start with your first breath-hold or arrive with depth already and refine it — every course is run in small numbers, at your pace.

AIDA 1Discover
A one-day introduction — breathing, relaxation and your first breath-hold dives in shallow, confined water. No experience needed.
to ~10m
AIDA 2Foundation
The complete foundation — relaxation, equalisation, finning technique, buddy safety and rescue. The real start of depth freediving.
to ~20m
AIDA 3Advanced
Free immersion, the first mouthfill, refined technique and longer dives — building the toolkit to keep descending comfortably.
to ~30m
AIDA 4Master
Advanced equalisation, packing, and the calm of real depth — for divers committing to the deep end of the sport.
to ~40m
InstructorTeach
For divers ready to teach — instructor development and crossover, mentored by a Master Instructor. By application.

III · 1:1 Coaching

Coaching built
around you.

Beyond the set courses, Harry takes on a small number of divers for ongoing one-to-one coaching — your technique, your goals, your timeline. It starts with a discovery call to understand where you are and where you want to get to.

  • A plan shaped around your current depth and goals
  • Video review, dive analysis and personal feedback
  • Dryland support and breath-work between sessions
  • Competition prep and dive planning when you're ready

IV · Depth camps

Regular depth camps
in Dominica.

Several times a year, Harry runs small-group depth camps in Soufrière Bay — one of the world's premier depth-training locations. No waves, no current, no boat ride — just deep, warm, sheltered water a few fin-kicks from shore.

  • WhereSoufrière Bay, Dominica — sheltered, volcanic, deep right off the shore
  • WhoAIDA 3+ divers and competitors working on depth, in small groups
  • WaterWarm and still year-round · over 100 m within swimming distance
  • CoachingDaily coached line sessions, video review, dive planning and safety
